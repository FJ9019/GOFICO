Values

Governance, Finance and Accounting, known by the acronym « GOFICO » is renowned for its thoroughness, professionalism and customer focus. It provides its services with due diligence and in compliance with specifications and contracts.

Mission

GOFICO's mission is to build the capacity of civil society organizations, women's groups, local authorities and SMEs.

    Vision

  • Promote wealth-creating SMEs and CBOs/CSOs that can make a lasting contribution to the country's socio-economic development.
